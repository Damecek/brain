# Brain Wiki

A personal knowledge base maintained by an LLM. The human adds sources,
asks questions, and curates. The LLM processes, connects, and maintains.

## How to Use

**Add knowledge:** Drop files into `sources/` (articles, papers, book
excerpts, notes, transcripts) and ask the maintainer to ingest them.
You can also paste content directly.

**Ask questions:** Ask anything. The maintainer will answer from the
wiki's contents, cite sources, and note gaps. Substantial answers get
filed as analysis pages.

**Browse:** Use Obsidian's graph view to explore connections. Use the
[[index]] to find specific pages. Use Dataview queries to filter and sort.

**Health check:** Ask the maintainer to lint the wiki. It will find
orphan pages, broken links, stale content, and un-ingested sources.
